The story

The beginning…
Sindhu and Amrit met in the Fall of 2010 at the beginning of their Sophomore year of college. Both attended the University of Illinois and were heavily involved in the two largest South Asian dance teams on campus, which allowed them to get to know each other much better. They say that the best of love stories blossom out of the best of friendships, and that’s exactly what happened between Sindhu and Amrit. Over the course of their sophomore year, the two developed a strong friendship. It was during the summer between their sophomore and junior years of college that they realized what was between them was more than a friendship. After dancing around each other, quite literally, for almost three months they decided to make things official in the winter months of 2010.

The proposal...
In the fall of 2015, Amrit decided it was time. After carefully picking out the perfect ring and planning for weeks, on September 15th, 2015, Amrit got down on one knee and asked Sindhu to marry him. With the help of both his own and Sindhu’s families, he was able to create the most perfect proposal. The special event happened at the gazebo near Sindhu’s home where the couple spent many hours getting to know each other at the beginning of their relationship. Along with Gautham, his sister and cousin, Amrit decorated the gazebo with beautiful drapes, balloons, blankets and candles. When everything was ready, he called Sindhu in a frenzy with news of a flat tire. Sindhu came rushing over to help, only to find that there was no flat tire after all. She then walked into the gazebo to the most amazing surprise, and with that, the couple was engaged.
